About Me

Hello! I'm Jaric Mosley S. Calderon, 19-year-old from Maypajo, Caloocan City. I was born and raised in Tondo, Manila, a place that has shaped me into who I am today. Currently, I'm pursuing my passion for computers at Global Reciprocal College in Caloocan City, where I'm studying for a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT). I've always been fascinated by computers and technology. It's more than just a hobby for me - it's a passion that drives me. That's why I chose IT as my field of study. I love the challenge of solving complex problems and the satisfaction that comes with finding a solution. The world of IT is vast and ever-evolving, and I'm excited to be a part of it. Whether it's software development, cybersecurity, data analysis, or web developer, I'm eager to explore all that this field has to offer. I believe that with hard work, dedication, and a never-ending desire to learn, I can make a significant contribution to the IT industry. I'm looking forward to the journey ahead and can't wait to see where my passion for computers will take me. Wish me luck!

Hobbies & Interest

Playing Video Games

Playing video games isn't just something I do for fun, it's an escape where I can freely express myself and unleash my creativity without any limits. Whether I'm on an exciting adventure or battling friends in multiplayer games, gaming gives me a sense of control and excitement that keeps me hooked for hours.

Travelling

One more thing I love doing is traveling to far-off spots. I do it because I feel calm surrounded by beautiful views and peaceful settings. Each trip teaches me how cool it is to learn about new cultures and talk to folks who see life in different ways.

Gadgets

I'm interested in gadgets because they can make things easier, faster, and more fun. They're handy for entertainment, making life convenient, and staying in touch with others. Gadgets just make everything more enjoyable and simpler for me.

Watching Movies/Anime

Watching anime and sci-fi movies is another hobby of mine because they offer a unique experience that feels totally different from reality. It adds an intense and exciting flavor to my life, transporting me to worlds filled with endless imagination and adventure. Each story and character resonates with me in a way that's both captivating and exhilarating, making it a truly enjoyable pastime.

Puzzle solving

Solving puzzles, especially the Rubik's Cube, is a hobby I enjoy because it feels like it sharpens my ability to think quickly. It's like exercising my brain and improving my problem-solving skills in a fun way. Plus, the satisfaction of solving the puzzle is incredibly rewarding and keeps me coming back for more challenges.

Adruino

I'm interested in learning Arduino because it allows me to create many projects and gadgets. It's a way for me to enhance my skills and work on different ideas I have in mind. Plus, the ability to build things with Arduino seems like a valuable skill that I'd like to develop further.
